Dave's Movies

A List of Movies That I've Enjoyed Watching More Than Once

The Death of Stalin

An historically accurate but quickily funny movie about the transition from Stalin to his successors.

The Aviator

Fascinating and well-acted true story of Howard Hughes life.

Catch Me If You Can

Also largely true story of a man who is able to kite hundreds of checks by pretending to be, among other things, an airline pilot.
